Gyris will be more than a beautifully crafted NFT project. Our vision is to become the world’s first web3 metaverse film and gaming franchise.

Story

As the gluttonous Minjierri slaughter the creatures of Gyris, the brave Bura come to their aid. While this great war ensues a reluctant hero rises. Argus succumbs to the call of the Duali, the ancient tradition where Bura youth take a perilous quest in search of their fated ‘Dua’. These weapons are said to be divined by the gods themselves. But darkness seeks flesh beyond the battlefield and evil takes root in his dreams. If Argus is to become the future of his people he must learn; that no one alone can fight the dark.

Follow Argus, Saras, Djalu and the inhabitants of Gyris in a saga spanning 3 generations. No one alone, can fight the Dark.
