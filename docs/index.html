Gyris will be more than a beautifully crafted NFT project. Our vision is to become the world’s first web3 metaverse film and gaming franchise.

‘Gyris: The Sound of Trees Standing’ delivers a narrative driven metaverse project with truly deep narrative. A world-first experiment in collaborative creativity and ownership, the team will release storyline and art while funding infrastructure and opportunities that reward a community of holder-artists. As an artist or a fan, you will be asked to colour our rich story with your own art and voice and together build the tapestry to best depict our world.

Story

As the gluttonous Minjierri slaughter the creatures of Gyris, the brave Bura come to their aid. While this great war ensues, a reluctant hero must rise. Argus succumbs to the call of the ancient tradition of ‘Duali’. Young Bura take a perilous quest in search of their fated ‘Dua’, weapons said to be divined by the gods themselves. But darkness seeks flesh beyond the battlefield and evil takes root in his dreams. If Argus is to become the future of his people he must learn; that no one alone can fight the dark.

Follow Argus, Saras, Djalu and the inhabitants of Gyris in a saga spanning 3 generations. No one alone, can fight the Dark.
