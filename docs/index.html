Gaming

We want to bring the beautiful world of Gyris to life and we chose Gaming as the first medium to do so. This way we can keep developing high-quality 3D assets for the game and have them at hand for once the movies and series are ready to be made. Furthermore, gaming is a beautiful way to experience and interact with a creative behemoth like Gyris: full of races, creatures and stories to be told.

Our main release is planned to be new type of social MMORPG experience. A way for which, will be paved by releasing multiple AAA quality Minigames. This gives us ability to engage with our community and show development progress, while we are slowly building all these 3D assets and game mechanics in Unreal as the main release - MMORPG will take great amounts of resources and time.

Game map of Buralands

Buralands is the home to one of our 4 playable races - The Bura. It alone is bigger than 2500 km2 and phase 1 will accommodate more than 200 villages and 11,000 plots of land.

Inspired by North Western Australia and Papua New Guinean geography we find dense forest, high mountains, deep cuts and valleys. Thunderous rivers but also endless branches of creek forks cutting lines in the mountainside. Families carve paths into the earthen clay and form walkways from their hut to the waterways to wash and bath and fish.
