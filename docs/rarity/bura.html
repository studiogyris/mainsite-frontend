Memorandum I entry:

A healer from the Duara tribe, wise and resilient, known for his kindness, bravery, and a very deep, soothing voice. Nicknamed as Hadringya - “Healer of Souls”, he has made himself popular and a respected Bura among the tribe by attacking a Minjierri camp at night, to save his defeated and wounded Burakin readied for execution. Outnumbered, managed to overpower the enemy squad at the camp and heal his Burakin, one of which was his older brother [101]. He dragged three wounded brethren a few hundred feet away from the camp and covered them with leaves, in hopes of camouflaging them. Only able to drag his wounded self, he hurried to gather two more strong men who helped him get his fellow Burakin home. Results of this brave act was turning the tides of the battle: his brethren had overheard the locations of the other nearby camps from the conversations of the Minjierri soldiers…

The most important outcome for him was, of course, the life of his older brother.
