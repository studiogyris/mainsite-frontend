Rarity and Scoring

Rarity in Gyris is interpreted slightly differently, rather than judging value purely by multiplying the chances for rolling specific traits we decided to split the scoring in two: visual score and a non-visual score ( a.k.a Base rating ) and by combining the two one would get a value for relative comparison of two unique items from the same collection.

Visual scoring

Instead of giving each trait a visual score based on it’s chance for rolling, we assigned each trait to a Rarity Tier. The tiers and their naming will be familiar to anyone who has played RPG games: Common, Uncommon , Rare , Epic , Legendary and Divine. Each tier is given score/value points and the final visual score is the sum of all traits. You can see the detailed scoring and tier of each trait here.
